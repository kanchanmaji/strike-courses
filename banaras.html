Mystical Banaras

Trip from Jamtara

The Spiritual Capital of India

A perfectly crafted 3-day budget itinerary to experience the Ghats, the Aarti, and the ancient alleyways of Kashi.

15

The Departure

  • 11:00 PM: Board Sleeper Train from Jamtara (JMT).

16

  • 06:15 PM: Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh.

Sunrise, Boat & Sarnath

  • 05:45 AM: Subah-e-Banaras (Assi Ghat).
  • Boat ride to Dashashwamedh.
  • Sarnath Excursion (Till 1:30 PM).
  • Evening cafe hopping at Assi.
18

Temples, BHU & Return

  • Shop for Wooden Toys & Rudraksha.
  • 06:00 PM: Return Train.
